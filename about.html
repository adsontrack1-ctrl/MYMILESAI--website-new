About

Mileage tracking that respects you.

We built MyMilesAI because every professional who drives deserves an app that's accurate, private, and affordable.

What we do

MyMilesAI is a mileage tracking app for the United States and Canada. It automatically records your business trips using GPS, classifies them as business or personal using AI, and calculates your tax deductions in real time based on the current IRS rate ($0.70/mile) or CRA rate ($0.72/km).

When tax season comes, you export a compliant mileage report as PDF or CSV and send it to your accountant. No manual logging, no forgotten trips, no scrambling in April.

Why we built it

We built MyMilesAI because the existing mileage trackers on the market had three problems that frustrated us:

🔒
Privacy concerns
Most apps upload your GPS data to their servers. We believe your location is your business — literally. MyMilesAI stores everything on your device.
📡
Unreliable tracking
Missed trips, split drives, and apps that don't work without internet. MyMilesAI uses Smart Pause GPS logic and works fully offline.
💸
Overpriced plans
Competitors charge $8.99/month for basic tracking. MyMilesAI Pro is $4.99/month with more features, and includes a free tier forever.

How it works

MyMilesAI runs in the background on your phone. When you start driving, it detects motion and begins logging your trip automatically — no buttons to press, no app to open.

As you drive, the app records your route, distance, speed, and duration using native GPS. When you stop, the trip is saved and classified by the AI engine as business or personal based on your patterns (time of day, destination frequency, work hours).

You can always override the classification with a simple swipe. At any time, you can see your running deduction total, view your trip history on a map, and export IRS or CRA-compliant reports.
